Frequently Asked Questions

What is a forex trend tracker?

A forex trend tracker is a tool that shows whether a currency pair is currently trending up or down, and how strong that trend is. The Pips & Co. Trend Tracker uses rate of change analysis across multiple timeframes to give traders a quick, clear view of market direction.

How do I use multi-timeframe analysis?

Multi-timeframe analysis involves looking at the same currency pair across multiple timeframes to identify alignment. For example, if USDJPY is trending up on the daily, weekly, and monthly charts, that is a strong signal that the broader trend is bullish. Traders then look for pullbacks on lower timeframes such as the 1-hour or 4-hour chart to find entry points in the direction of the higher timeframe trend.

Is this trend tracker free?

Yes, the Pips & Co. Trend Tracker is completely free with no registration or sign-up required.

What are Deriv synthetic indices?

Deriv synthetic indices are proprietary instruments offered by Deriv that simulate market movements with consistent volatility 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. They are not affected by real-world news events. Popular synthetic indices include Volatility 10, Volatility 25, Volatility 50, Volatility 75, Volatility 100, Crash 500, Crash 1000, Boom 500, and Boom 1000.
